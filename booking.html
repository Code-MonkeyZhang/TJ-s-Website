Frequently Asked Questions

What should I prepare for the consultation?

It's helpful to have a general understanding of your current financial processes and any specific challenges you're facing. There's no need to prepare formal documents for the initial consultation.

How long does the consultation last?

Our initial consultations are scheduled for 30 minutes. If we need more time, we can arrange a follow-up call.

Is the consultation really free?

Yes, the initial consultation is completely free with no obligation. We believe in demonstrating our value before asking for your business.

What happens after the consultation?

Following the consultation, we'll send you a summary of our discussion along with our recommendations and a quote if applicable. You'll have time to review this information before making any decisions.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can easily cancel or reschedule through the confirmation email you receive after booking. We appreciate at least 24 hours notice if possible.
