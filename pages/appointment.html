Frequently Asked Questions

What should I prepare for the consultation?

For the most productive consultation, please have recent financial statements, tax returns, and a list of your financial questions or concerns ready.

How long does the initial consultation last?

Initial consultations typically last 20 minutes, which is sufficient time to discuss your needs and determine next steps.

Is there a fee for the initial consultation?

The initial 20-minute consultation is complimentary. We believe in understanding your needs before proposing any services.

Can I reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule through the confirmation email you received or by contacting our office directly at 412-438-8448.
